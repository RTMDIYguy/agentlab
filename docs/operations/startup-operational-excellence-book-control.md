## Production Requirements

The book will move into Calibre 64 for formatting, metadata management, cover
handling, and export preparation.

Production constraints:

- metadata must match across manuscript, Calibre, cover, graphics, and KDP
- keep review copies in both DOCX and PDF formats for human readers before
  upload
- graphics must use a consistent style and include source notes
- graphics must carry alt text or readable captions for ebook accessibility
- AI-assisted graphics must be disclosed
- KDP requirements must be rechecked before final export
- formatting should be tested before KDP upload to reduce upload / adjust /
  re-upload cycles
- final production folder must contain only clean production assets

Working assumptions:

- primary edition: Kindle eBook
- secondary edition: paperback after manuscript stabilizes
- paperback trim: 8.5" x 11", matched to the shipped `The Bootstrappers Guide
  to the World` PDF/DOCX
- print margins: 0.75" top/bottom and 0.5" left/right, matched to the shipped
  Bootstrapper DOCX
- cover can be produced through Calibre or Canva, with the final cover asset
  checked against Amazon KDP requirements before upload
- human review packet should include matching DOCX and PDF copies generated
  from the same manuscript version
- series: none; this is a stand-alone book
- categories: Business Startup; Bootstrapping, matched to the shipped
  Bootstrapper Calibre OPF subjects and mapped to closest KDP categories during
  setup
- keywords: business startup; bootstrapping; startup operational excellence;
  SOE; StartupOperationalExcellence; small business systems; founder
  operations; operational excellence; Ownable

Positioning note:

Robert confirmed on 2026-05-20 that this book predates his reading of
`Ownable OS`. Its fit as a prequel should be treated as organic and
providential, not as a planned series relationship or derivative claim.

Pre-upload quality gate:

1. Confirm metadata is consistent across DOCX, PDF, Calibre, cover, graphics,
   and Amazon detail-page fields.
2. Confirm all graphics have source notes, captions or alt text, and no fake
   client data.
3. Confirm DOCX and PDF review copies are generated from the same manuscript
   version.
4. Confirm ebook formatting, table of contents, headings, image placement, and
   page breaks in Calibre / Kindle Previewer or KDP Previewer.
5. Confirm paperback trim, margins, bleed, cover dimensions, and spine rules
   before uploading print files.
6. Upload to Amazon only after the review packet and preview checks pass.
