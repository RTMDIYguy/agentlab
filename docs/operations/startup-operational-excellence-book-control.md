getting lost in the broader Agent Lab / URC project stack.

service-business founder, under his wing and teaches him how to build a
business that can grow without losing its soul.

## Approved Creative Direction

- Mentor: Uncle Robert
- Composite founder: Sam
- Sam represents an early-stage or small service business owner with too many
  tools, too little clarity, and a growing understanding that structure is not
  the enemy of creativity.
- Voice: Uncle Robert Consulting voice, approved by Robert on 2026-05-18.
- Narrative pattern: scene, mentor conversation, principle, practice,
  checkpoint, narrative return, takeaway.
- Disclosure standard: include composite, AI-assisted writing, and AI-assisted
  graphics disclosures.
