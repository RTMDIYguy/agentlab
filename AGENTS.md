## Core Rules

- Do not redesign the business from zero.
- Prefer implementation over brainstorming.
- Preserve existing business data.
- Use `Microsoft 365` as the default operating backbone.
- Use the current Microsoft 365 finance tracker as the finance control layer while revenue is small; do not assume paid finance tools are worth keeping.
- Use `Notion` sparingly as a lightweight dashboard layer only.
- Treat the book as an authority and funnel asset.
- Keep `URC` as the main business brand.
- Keep `Bootstrapper Capital` as the audience and event funnel.
- Keep `Tactix` as the fulfillment and Upwork arm.
- Prefer low-cost and free routes wherever possible.
- Automate first, review exceptions, and use paid human help only where judgment, compliance, or leverage truly requires it.
- Keep the main execution focus on testing, certifying, packaging, and taking workflows to market.
