## Description Draft

Most founders do not fail because they lack ideas. They struggle because the business in their head grows faster than the systems underneath it.

`Startup Operational Excellence` is a practical mentor guide for founders who are building with limited time, limited money, and real customers waiting on real promises. Through the story of Sam, a composite small-business founder, Robert T. McCarthy shows how to turn scattered tools, unclear handoffs, missed follow-ups, and founder exhaustion into a simple operating rhythm a business can actually live inside.

This book is not about becoming corporate. It is about building a company with enough structure to protect its soul.

Inside, readers will learn how to map customer promises, separate value from motion, review tool spend, protect cash, build learning loops, create a weekly operating cadence, and recognize the bootstrap limit wall before it stops momentum. The goal is not perfection. The goal is a business that is truthful, trustworthy, teachable, and able to carry its promises beyond the founder's memory, mood, and exhaustion.
