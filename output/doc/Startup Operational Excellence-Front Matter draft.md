# How To Use This Book

This book is meant to be read like a mentor conversation and used like a field
guide.

You do not have to fix everything at once. In fact, trying to fix everything
at once is one of the ways founders stay stuck. Read for clarity first. Mark
the places where your business feels familiar. When a chapter gives you a
checkpoint, choose one visible improvement and make that improvement real.

The appendices at the back are not homework for perfectionists. They are tools
for founders who need a place to start. Use them when the story names a
problem you recognize and you are ready to turn that recognition into action.
