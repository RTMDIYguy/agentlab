## Current Lightweight Thresholds

- Green: open rate at or above 35%, any qualified reply or booked call, no spam complaints.
- Yellow: open rate below 35% but no compliance issue; revise subject line or segment before scaling.
- Red: spam complaint, repeated bounces, wrong-company evidence, or confusing CTA; pause and clean the list.
